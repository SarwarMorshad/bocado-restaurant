About BOCADO

Founded by chef Lucía Ramos, BOCADO brings the flavors of Spain to the city with a focus on fresh, local ingredients and a welcoming atmosphere. Our team is passionate about sharing the joy of tapas and Spanish hospitality.

We source sustainably and support local producers whenever possible.
