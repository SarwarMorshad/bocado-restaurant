Privacy Policy

BOCADO (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This notice explains what we collect on this site and why.

Data we collect

  • Booking details: name, email, party size, date/time, occasion, notes.
  • Technical data: IP address, device/browser info, and basic analytics (aggregated).

How we use your data

  • To process and confirm your booking and contact you about it.
  • To improve our website and service quality.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with service providers strictly to operate this website (e.g., hosting, analytics, email provider) under data-processing agreements, and when required by law.

Retention

Booking data is kept only as long as needed for reservations, legal, and accounting purposes.

Your rights

Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use only essential and basic analytics cookies. You can control cookies in your browser settings.
